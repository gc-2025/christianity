Causes of Snow Leopard Population Decline

Many factors contribute to their declining numbers in these countries. Some of the top causes are:

  1. Climate change - greatest long term threat that could destroy 30% of their habitat
  2. Local communities - kill the animals that snow leopards prey upon
  3. Killed by farmers - when they are forced to prey on livestock to survive
  4. Human settlement in their range - forces them to survive in smaller areas with less prey
